Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favourite
websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can
access them on the go.

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag
-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you
manage your favourite sites

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you find
saved sites in no time at all. No need to
crawl through all of your bookmarks.

Share your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with others.
Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of a
button.
